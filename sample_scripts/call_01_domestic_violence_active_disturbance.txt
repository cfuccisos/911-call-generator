	1.	Domestic violence / active disturbance
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 1428 Pine Ridge Drive, Apartment 3B. Please hurry.
Dispatcher: Tell me exactly what’s happening.
Caller: My boyfriend is screaming and throwing things. He shoved me into the wall.
Dispatcher: Are you hurt?
Caller: My arm hurts, but I can move it.
Dispatcher: Is he there with you right now?
Caller: Yes—he’s in the living room yelling.
Dispatcher: Are there any weapons in the home?
Caller: He has a handgun in the bedroom drawer.
Dispatcher: Stay on the line. Can you get to a room with a lock?
Caller: I’m in the bathroom now. The door locks.
Dispatcher: Good. Keep the door locked and stay quiet. Officers are on the way.
