	9.	Hit-and-run
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 300 block of Lakeshore Avenue.
Dispatcher: What’s going on?
Caller: A car just hit a parked vehicle and sped off.
Dispatcher: Did anyone get hurt?
Caller: No injuries that I saw, but it crushed the bumper.
Dispatcher: Can you describe the fleeing vehicle?
Caller: Blue sedan, maybe a Toyota, left taillight broken. Partial plate: 7KX—something.
Dispatcher: Which direction did it go?
Caller: North on Lakeshore toward the bridge.
Dispatcher: Officers will check the area. Please remain available for a statement.
