	3.	Suspicious person / prowler
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 2300 West Elm Street, behind the Walgreens.
Dispatcher: What’s going on there?
Caller: There’s a man walking between cars, peeking inside windows.
Dispatcher: Is he trying door handles?
Caller: Yes, he just tried mine and moved to the next row.
Dispatcher: Are you in your vehicle right now?
Caller: Yes, I’m sitting inside with the doors locked.
Dispatcher: Good. Do not confront him. Can you describe him?
Caller: Gray beanie, black jacket, backpack, maybe late 30s.
Dispatcher: Officers are on the way. Keep your doors locked.
