	2.	Burglary in progress
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 77 Meadowbrook Lane. Someone’s breaking into my neighbor’s house—maybe mine next!
Dispatcher: Tell me what you see.
Caller: A guy in a dark hoodie trying the back door with something like a crowbar.
Dispatcher: Is he inside yet?
Caller: Not yet—he just forced the door open!
Dispatcher: Are you in a safe place?
Caller: I’m inside with my doors locked.
Dispatcher: Good. Do not go outside. Can you describe him further?
Caller: Medium build, about 6 feet, dark hoodie, jeans, white sneakers.
Dispatcher: Officers are being dispatched now. Stay where you are.
