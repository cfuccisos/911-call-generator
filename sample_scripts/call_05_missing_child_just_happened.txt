	5.	Missing child (just happened)
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 910 Maple Court—my son is missing.
Dispatcher: How old is he?
Caller: He’s 6. His name is Ethan.
Dispatcher: When did you last see him?
Caller: Five minutes ago—he was in the backyard.
Dispatcher: What is he wearing?
Caller: A red T-shirt, gray shorts, and blue sneakers.
Dispatcher: Any nearby water—pools, ponds, creeks?
Caller: There’s a small pond behind the fence line.
Dispatcher: Start checking that area immediately while staying on the line—help is being dispatched.
