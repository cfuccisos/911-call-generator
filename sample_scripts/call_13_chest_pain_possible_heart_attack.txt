	13.	Chest pain / possible heart attack
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 66 Oak Terrace. My dad is having chest pain.
Dispatcher: How old is he?
Caller: 58.
Dispatcher: Is he awake and breathing?
Caller: Yes, but he’s sweating and says his arm hurts.
Dispatcher: Has he ever had heart problems?
Caller: Yes—stents last year.
Dispatcher: Is the pain severe, and when did it start?
Caller: About 10 minutes ago and it’s getting worse.
Dispatcher: EMS is on the way. Have him sit upright, stay calm, and unlock the door if you can.
