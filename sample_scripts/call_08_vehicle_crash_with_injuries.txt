	8.	Vehicle crash with injuries
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: Interstate 95 southbound, mile marker 142—there’s a crash!
Dispatcher: What happened?
Caller: Two cars and an SUV. The SUV is on its side.
Dispatcher: Any fire or smoke?
Caller: No fire, but there’s leaking fluid.
Dispatcher: How many people injured?
Caller: At least two—one person is trapped in the SUV, I think.
Dispatcher: Do not approach leaking fluids. Are you in a safe spot?
Caller: Yes, pulled over on the shoulder with hazards on.
Dispatcher: Help is being sent. Stay on the line and keep a safe distance.
