	12.	Overdose (opioid) / breathing problems
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 901 South 3rd Street, in the alley behind the apartments.
Dispatcher: What’s the problem?
Caller: A man is slumped over. He’s barely breathing.
Dispatcher: Is he awake?
Caller: No. I shook him and he didn’t respond.
Dispatcher: Are his lips turning blue or gray?
Caller: Yes, kind of blue.
Dispatcher: Do you have naloxone/Narcan available?
Caller: I do—I have a kit.
Dispatcher: I’ll guide you. While I stay on the line, start giving it per the instructions and tell me when it’s administered.
