	16.	Allergic reaction / anaphylaxis
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 88 Garden View Lane. My daughter got stung and she can’t breathe well.
Dispatcher: How old is she?
Caller: 14.
Dispatcher: Is she wheezing or swelling in the face/lips?
Caller: Yes—her lips are swelling.
Dispatcher: Do you have an EpiPen?
Caller: Yes, we do.
Dispatcher: Use it now per the label. EMS is on the way—tell me once it’s given.
