	17.	Seizure (medical)
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 2500 North Park, near the playground.
Dispatcher: What’s happening?
Caller: A man just fell and he’s shaking—like a seizure.
Dispatcher: Is he on the ground?
Caller: Yes.
Dispatcher: Don’t restrain him. Clear objects away and turn him on his side when safe. Is he breathing?
Caller: I think so—yes.
Dispatcher: Good. Tell me how long it lasts. EMS is on the way.
