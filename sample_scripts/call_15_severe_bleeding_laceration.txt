	15.	Severe bleeding / laceration
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 12 Harbor Road—my husband cut his hand badly.
Dispatcher: Is the bleeding controlled?
Caller: No, it’s pouring.
Dispatcher: Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth. Is he awake?
Caller: Yes, but he’s pale and shaking.
Dispatcher: Keep pressure and elevate the hand above the heart if possible. EMS is on the way.
