	20.	Civil standby / keep the peace
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 1010 Crestview Drive. I need an officer to be here while I pick up my things.
Dispatcher: Tell me more about the situation.
Caller: I’m leaving my ex’s house. He’s not supposed to contact me, but I need my clothes and paperwork.
Dispatcher: Is he there right now?
Caller: He is, and I’m worried he’ll start yelling.
Dispatcher: Any weapons known at the address?
Caller: He owns a rifle, but it’s usually locked up.
Dispatcher: Understood. Officers can do a standby. Stay in your vehicle outside until they arrive.
