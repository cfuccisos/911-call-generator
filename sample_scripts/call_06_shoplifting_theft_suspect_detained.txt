	6.	Shoplifting / theft suspect detained
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 5150 Commerce Plaza, the Target—I’m security.
Dispatcher: What’s the situation?
Caller: We have a shoplifter detained in the office. He’s getting aggressive.
Dispatcher: Any weapons seen?
Caller: Not seen, but he keeps reaching into his pockets.
Dispatcher: How many people involved?
Caller: Just him, one security officer with me.
Dispatcher: Keep distance and do not search him if it’s unsafe. Officers are en route.
