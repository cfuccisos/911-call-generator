	14.	Stroke symptoms
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: 404 Sunset Drive. My mom is acting strange.
Dispatcher: Tell me what you’re seeing.
Caller: Her face is drooping on one side and her speech is slurred.
Dispatcher: When did this start?
Caller: Just now—maybe 5 minutes ago.
Dispatcher: Can she raise both arms?
Caller: One arm keeps dropping.
Dispatcher: This could be a stroke. EMS is being dispatched now. Note the time symptoms started—stay on the line.
