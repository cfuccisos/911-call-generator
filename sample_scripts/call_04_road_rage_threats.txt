	4.	Road rage / threats
Dispatcher: 911 what’s the location of your emergency
Caller: I’m on Route 16 eastbound near the River Street exit.
Dispatcher: What’s happening?
Caller: A pickup truck is following me, swerving at me. The driver just pointed something at me—maybe a gun!
Dispatcher: What’s your vehicle description?
Caller: Silver Honda Accord, and the truck is a black Ford F-150 with a dented tailgate.
Dispatcher: Do not stop. Can you safely continue driving?
Caller: Yes, but he’s right on my bumper.
Dispatcher: Stay in the right lane if you can and keep driving toward a public area. Officers are being notified.
